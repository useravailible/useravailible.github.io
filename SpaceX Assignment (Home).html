Introduction to SpaceX



SpaceX is a privately held company founded by Elon Musk in 2002. The main focus of SpaceX is to reduce space transportation costs and to enable the colonization of Mars.

SpaceX has been successful in designing and building reusable rockets and spacecrafts and launching them into space. This breakthrough of SpaceX has significantly reduced the cost of space exploration.

In this assignment, we will focus on the aspects of SpaceX that are relevant to the users of the financial reports and that can be represented faithfully.
